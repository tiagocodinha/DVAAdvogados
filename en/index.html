About Us

Duarte, Vieira & Associates, RL is a law firm established in 2006.

The firm and its partners advise both Portuguese and international companies and individuals, delivering full-service support across several areas of Portuguese law. With over 10 years of experience in business law, DVA Advogados fields a multidisciplinary team assisting companies from tax advisory and company formation to all legal steps required to operate in the Portuguese market.

We advise major corporations, investors, financial institutions, and individuals across Europe, Asia, the United States, Angola, and Brazil, in multiple sectors of the economy. Our current expansion plan includes opening an office in Hong Kong.

For those wishing to live in Portugal, we help clients obtain Golden Visas (residence permits by investment) as well as all other types of residence permits and Non-Habitual Resident status.

Clients have direct and easy access to senior partners and the team, enabling us to gain a deep understanding of their businesses and provide efficient, well-structured, and tailored solutions—building strong and long-lasting relationships.

Our commitment is to deliver a service of excellence based on honesty, trust, transparency, efficiency, and competence.

Services

We have proven experience supporting investors seeking to establish themselves in Portugal. A dedicated group of specialists in immigration, tax, real estate, succession, and corporate law enables us to offer truly personalized guidance to each client.

Golden Visa

Portugal offers exceptional tax and immigration conditions for foreign nationals establishing themselves in the country, with special programs such as the Non-Habitual Resident regime and the Golden Visa in high demand.

Company Formation

We represent and assist clients of every size and sector in incorporations, mergers and acquisitions, corporate restructurings (judicial and extra-judicial), corporate governance, commercial and contract matters, as well as negotiating partnerships and joint ventures.

Bank Accounts

Our Banking & Finance team advises on opening bank accounts in Portugal for foreign individuals or companies, with the speed and accuracy your business requires.

Other Areas of Expertise

Immigration Law

Full advisory on residence permits, nationality, and visas

Real Estate Law

Support in real estate transactions and investments

Tax Law

Tax consulting and international tax planning

We also advise foreign clients seeking to acquire Portuguese nationality or to immigrate under other residence categories, such as work, highly-qualified activity, passive income, student, start-up, retiree, or entrepreneur permits.

Our service includes rigorous organization, review, and submission of residence applications to the competent authorities, and we accompany the client throughout every stage of the investment, from structuring to international succession planning.

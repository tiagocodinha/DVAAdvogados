About Us

Duarte, Vieira e Associados, RL is a law firm established in 2006.

The firm and its partners have been assisting both corporate and private clients, national and international, providing comprehensive services across multiple areas of Portuguese law. With over 15 years of experience in corporate law, DVA Advogados brings together a multidisciplinary team, supporting companies from tax advisory and company incorporation to all legal steps required to operate in the Portuguese market.

We advise major corporations, investors, financial institutions and individuals across Europe, Asia, the United States, Angola and Brazil, in a wide range of industries. The firm’s current expansion includes opening an office in Hong Kong.

For those seeking to live in Portugal, we support clients in obtaining Golden Visas (residence by investment), as well as all types of residence permits and the Non-Habitual Resident status.

Clients have direct access to senior partners and our team, allowing us to gain a deep understanding of their businesses and deliver efficient, structured and truly bespoke solutions, building strong and long-lasting relationships.

Our commitment is to deliver excellence based on honesty, trust, transparency, efficiency and competence.

Services

Our firm has proven experience supporting investors who wish to establish themselves in Portugal. A specialised group of lawyers in immigration, tax, real estate, succession and corporate law allows us to provide personalised, end-to-end assistance to each client.

Portugal offers highly attractive tax and immigration conditions for foreign residents, with the Golden Visa and the Non-Habitual Resident regime among the most sought-after programmes.

We assist clients of all sizes and sectors with incorporation, mergers and acquisitions, corporate restructurings, corporate governance, commercial and contract matters, as well as the negotiation of partnerships and joint ventures.

Our Banking & Finance team provides assistance with opening bank accounts in Portugal for foreign individuals and companies, with the agility your business needs.

Comprehensive support with residence permits, nationality and visas.

Advisory on property transactions and investments.

Tax consulting and international tax planning.

We also assist foreign clients who wish to acquire Portuguese nationality or immigrate to Portugal through other residence grounds, such as work, qualified activity, passive income, students, startups, retirees or entrepreneurs.

Our services include the careful organisation, review and submission of residence applications to the competent authorities, and we accompany the client throughout every stage of their investment, from structuring to international succession planning.
